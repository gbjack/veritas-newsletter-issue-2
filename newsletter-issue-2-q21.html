Message From Gary
Hi Team,

FY20 was a monumental year for the APJ Channel & Alliances team. We have worked out how to measure success and be able to hold our heads up high while driving Channel Discrete and Channel Led business; we have further cemented our position by creating Channel Led GTM’s in each of our Regions and built out the Channel Leadership Team increasing our capabilities both Operationally & in Channel Marketing.


In FY20 we:
• Increased our Indirect Mix to 96% (the highest anywhere in the world)
• Increased our Channel Discrete and Channel Led Revenue to 54% of the overall APJ business (an increase from 51% going in to FY20)
• Increased revenue from Opportunity Registration by 19% YoY and our Pipeline from Opportunity Registrations by a whopping 249% YoY.
• Improved our utilization of VPDF with ROI trending at up to 28x
These are amazing results and I thank each and every one of you for all your efforts in making this happen. Now my attention turns to FY21 & the challenges that lie ahead. We will continue to face competition for our Partners’ attention, but we now have a new Partner Force Program focusing on rewarding Partners & Distributers who grow with us (Pay for Performance). We are improving efficiencies in our business from developing the right partner relationships and improving our Partner’s experience in being advocates of the Veritas brand.

When we first all began working from home, I was concerned on how we were all going to achieve everything that we needed to achieve to close off FY20 and prepare for FY21. Now having been in ‘lockdown’ for the past 2 months, I am no longer worried about that, you have handled this transition to remote contact wonderfully.

What I am concerned about now is what kind of a team we will be when restrictions are lifted in each of our countries. Personally, most of my days start before 7am and most evenings are spent on conference calls with Global – my working day has expanded and there is very little separation from my personal life and my work life (including weekends & public holidays).
Please don’t follow my example on this – try and separate you work life away from your personal life – you will be better for it. Focus on your life ‘away from work’ just as you did before, but now do in a virtual way using the technology available to you. When we all return to work in whatever the ‘new normal’ will be, we need to attack FY21 with enthusiasm and vigour.

Our journey continues as we have just begun showing what this team is capable of. Keep looking for innovation in what you do and share our achievements loudly and proudly. Please take care of yourselves, I look forward to seeing you all again in person soon and sharing in your future success. Go Team!!!
ASR ended FY20 on a high with +25% YoY growth in new business. Our Channel Team’s mission in FY20 was “Focus on Growth” and “Focus on Quality”, with the latter emphasizing on increasing productivity and quality of partners. To support these strategies, our team embarked on several initiatives, and achieved solid results which we will build upon in FY21 together with the company’s “Growth with Cloud” strategy.
FY20 was a difficult year for GCR, faced with trade disputes, unrest and chaos in Hong Kong, and now COVID-19 pandemic. Despite all these challenges, with the strong collaboration and support from partners, the team succeeded in building a solid foundation for the Channel business in FY20, which will continue to be built upon in FY21 with the strategies and activities mapped out.
 
India channel team ended FY20 with a strong channel ecosystem and exceptional growth. Like all other regions in APJ, India was hit by the difficulties due to COVID-19, but our team remained strong and focused in the goals we set out to achieve. Here are some of our achievements:
• Secured 9 Platinum, 8 Gold and 12 Silver partners
• Introduction of ISO team
• Built a strong distribution team with targeted distributed
• Introduced programmatic approach in enablement, demand generation and awareness initiatives
• Developed emerging countries with Channel partners
• Strong growth in incremental revenue delivered 74% YoY in Non‑Named segment
Japan’s progress was on track in FY20 Q3 with double digit growth. However, COVID-19 had and continues to have a big impact on the Japan business from Q4 onwards. Despite the immense challenges our team is facing, we have managed to achieve positive YoY growth in our New, Renewal and All-Up businesses in FY20, with a definitive strategy to maintain this success in FY21.
 
In FY20, the Korea team’s key Channel strategies were to set up a value-added distribution business, as well as to generate incremental revenue with our major resellers and Alliance partners. The team has executed these strategies successfully in FY20 with a host of meaningful results. FY21 will be no different as we mapped out the strategies and tasks we need to drive to have another successful year.
